What are payment fees?

                        
Payment fees are fees charged by a payment processor or other merchant platform when you make
                            a sale. Because payment fees are a cost of doing business online, you should take them into
                            account when estimating your net revenue. A few of the most popular payment processors and
                            online merchant platforms for startups are Stripe, Shopify, PayPal, Gumroad, and Amazon.
