What is NRR?

Net revenue retention (NRR) is the percentage of revenue retained from existing customers at the start of a period after accounting for expansion revenue and churn. NRR helps you understand how successful your company is at generating additional revenue from your existing customers. A high NRR indicates that your business is good at retention and customer success. A low NRR indicates that your business relies heavily on new customers.
